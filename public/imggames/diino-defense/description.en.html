🍄Diino Defense Guide for Kids: Easy Steps to Shield Every Egg

Jump right into Diino Defense, the colorful tower-defense game made just for young strategists! Your job is to stop waves of tiny monsters from stealing those precious Diino eggs. Here’s how to become the ultimate egg protector:

1. Pick and Place Your Towers

Look along the bottom toolbar to choose a type of tower—archer, cannon, or frost. Tap your favorite, then tap any empty tile beside the path. That’s where your tower will stand guard.

2. Earn Gold and Upgrade Quickly

Every time you defeat a creature, you’ll collect gold coins. Tap a tower you’ve built and then tap the upward arrow to invest your gold. Upgrades can increase firing speed, boost damage, or even freeze enemies in their tracks.

3. Mix Defenses for Maximum Power

Fast foes fear frost towers, while tanky monsters crumble under heavy cannons. Use a blend of tower styles so no baddie slips through your lines.

4. Watch the Wave Clock

A countdown shows when the next group of monsters will arrive. Fill any open spots before that timer hits zero—otherwise, sneaky critters might slip by!

5. Protect Every Single Egg

You start with a set number of lives. Each monster that reaches the nest costs you one life. If you run out, the game ends—so build smart, upgrade often, and keep those eggs safe!

Follow these simple steps, adapt your tactics as you go, and you’ll be the hero who saves every Diino egg in style!

Guide your adventurous Dino through glowing lava tunnels by tapping the ← and → arrow keys.

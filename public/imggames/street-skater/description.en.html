⚡How to Play Street Skater

Are you ready to roll on the coolest sidewalks ever? Here’s how you can become a street-skating superstar:

1. Hop on Your Board

Place one foot on the tail of your skateboard and push off with the other foot. Use the ← and → arrow keys to steer left or right as you cruise.

2. Ollie Over Obstacles

Tap the ↑ arrow key to jump over things like traffic cones, cardboard boxes, and puddles. Timing is very important—press jump just before you reach the obstacle.

3. Pull Off Tricks in Mid-Air

While you’re soaring through the air, hit the spacebar to spin, flip, or grind. Try out different combinations to find new moves!

4. Collect Coins and Stars

Golden coins will boost your score instantly. Shiny stars give you bonus points and help you unlock cool boards, helmets, and outfits.

5. Aim for the Top Score

Avoid everything in your path—crates, fire hydrants, even runaway cats! The higher your score, the faster you’ll unlock new gear and tougher levels.

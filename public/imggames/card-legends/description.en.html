How to Play Card Legend

Welcome to the exciting world of Card Legend! Begin your adventure by tapping the deck to shuffle your cards and deal yourself five. Choose any card from your hand and place it in the middle. Remember, higher numbers or special cards, like wizard hats, defeat the smaller ones. Each time you win a match, you earn a star!

Once you've collected five stars, you'll achieve the title of true Card Legend! If you run out of cards in your hand, simply press "Draw" to receive three new ones. Get ready to plan your moves carefully and enjoy each thrilling round!

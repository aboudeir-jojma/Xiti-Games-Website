📖 How to Play Ludo King

👋 Are you ready for some fun? Ludo King is family-friendly 🎲 where each player controls 4 colorful pawns. Here’s how:

1️⃣ Get Your Pawns Moving
🎲 Tap the dice. Rolling a six is lucky 🍀 because it lets you move a pawn onto the track.

2️⃣ Advance Around the Board
➡️ Move forward the spaces shown on the dice. Every roll takes you closer to the finish 🏁.

3️⃣ Knock Opponents Back
💥 Land on a rival and send their pawn back home 🏠. A clever move to stay ahead!

🔺 Circle the board and head into the center. You’ll need exact rolls to finish. The first player with all 4 pawns home wins 🏆!

How to Play Card Attack

Getting Started

First, each player—both you and the computer—starts with five cards and 20 health points. When it’s your turn, click the big “Draw” button to add a new card to your hand.

Your Turn

  1. Choose an Attack card to hit your opponent. The number on the card shows how many health points you will take away from them.
  2. After your attack, make sure you have only five cards in your hand by discarding any extras.

Computer’s Turn

Watch the screen as the computer draws a card and attacks. If the computer lands a hit, use a Shield card on your next turn to block any incoming damage and continue playing.

Winning the Game

Continue taking turns—drawing, attacking, and defending—until someone's health reaches zero. The player with health remaining is the winner! Are you ready to test your strategy and outsmart the computer? Let the Card Attack battle begin!

 

Controls

Guide your hero by pressing the left and right arrow keys. To leap over spikes or wide gaps, hit the up arrow or the spacebar. When a swinging trap comes down, tap the down arrow to crouch beneath it. See a gleaming card floating by? Click it with your mouse to add its special power to your hand.

Ready to make a move? Select your hero and click again to use the card against your foes. The best part of all is that Card Attack loads instantly in your web browser. No downloads or installations are needed, so you can jump into the excitement right away!

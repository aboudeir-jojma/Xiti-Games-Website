How to Play UNO Monument Edition

Are you ready for an exciting card adventure? Let's dive right in and learn to play!

1. Set Up Your Game

Shuffle all the cards thoroughly. Deal seven cards to each player. Place the remaining deck face-down in the center to create your draw pile. Flip the top card next to it to start the discard pile.

2. Taking Turns

On your turn, look at the top card on the discard pile. Play a card from your hand that matches its color, number, or symbol. Can't find a match? Draw one card from the draw pile and see if you can play it right away. If it still doesn't match, your turn is over.

3. Special Cards = Extra Fun

  • Reverse: Switches the order of play.
  • Skip: Skips the next player's turn.
  • Draw Two: The next player picks up two cards.
  • Wild: You can choose any color for the next play.
  • Wild Draw Four: You pick the new color, and the next player adds four cards to their hand!

4. Don't Forget to Shout “UNO!”

When you're down to your last card, shout “UNO!” loud and clear. If you forget and someone else catches you, you must draw two more cards!

5. Winning the Game

The goal is simple: be the first to get rid of all your cards. Once you play your last card, you're the UNO Monument Edition champion!

Tips for Super Play

  • Look out for what colors and numbers your friends are holding.
  • Save your Wild Draw Four for the perfect moment.
  • Have fun and celebrate with a victory dance when you win!

Now you're all set—let the colorful battles begin!

 

Controls

Are you ready to play UNO Monument Edition online? Here's how you can take charge of your cards right in your browser:

1. Left and Right Arrows: Move through your hand to choose the card you want to play.

2. Space Bar: Draw a new card from the deck whenever you need more options.

3. Enter Key: Once you find a card that matches in color or number, press Enter to place it on the discard pile.

4. U Key: When you are down to one card, press U and say "UNO!" to let everyone know you're almost out of cards.

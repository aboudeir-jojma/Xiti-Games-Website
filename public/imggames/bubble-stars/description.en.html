How to Play Bubble Stars

Ready to have some fun with Bubble Stars? Here’s how you can play:

1. Get Ready to Shoot

Tap and hold your bubble blaster at the bottom of the screen. Gently pull back to aim where you want the bubble to go.

2. Match and Pop

Release your finger to make your bubble soar! When three or more bubbles of the same color touch, they burst into sparkles!

3. Free the Shining Stars

Keep popping groups of bubbles until you clear the path above. That’s where the hidden stars are. Rescue them all to win the level!

4. Score Big with Combos

Aim for shots that cause one pop to lead to another. The more bubbles you burst in a row, the more bonus points you earn!

5. Have Fun and Explore

Each stage offers bright new designs and exciting challenges. Aim carefully, plan your moves, and watch those stars shine!
